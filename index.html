Calculate RevPAR

RevPAR, short for “revenue per available room” is the average revenue generated by each available guest room during a specific period of time. It is the industry standard KPI used to assess profitability in hotels.

You can use RevPAR to evaluate your own property’s revenue producing performance relative to that of your competitive set.
